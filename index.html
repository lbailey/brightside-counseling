Individual Counseling

Sessions typically last 45-50 minutes, although may be shorter for very young children. Modalities may include talk therapy or play therapy, depending on needs and age.

Relationship Counseling

Sessions usually last 45-50 minutes.
**Note: Often insurance companies will not pay for marriage therapy unless there is a very specific mental health diagnosis from one partner which is impacting your marriage; thus, things such as communication, infidelity, sexual incompatibility, and lifestyle incompatibility issues will not be covered under insurance. Please plan your finances accordingly.

Family Therapy

Sessions generally last 45-50 minutes. Modalities may include talk or play-based therapy, depending on age and needs of family members.

EMDR

Lacey is EMDR trained (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing), and EMDR is considered a specific treatment within individual therapy. EMDR has been proven to be helpful for trauma, substance abuse, eating disorders, depression, and anxiety. If Lacey feels this service would be good for you, she will recommend it.

Psychological Autopsy

Lacey is trained in psychological autopsies by the American Association of Suicidology. This training has given Lacey the knowledge to help families who have had a loved one complete suicide. Lacey helps trace back behavioral patterns and concerns in the loved one's life, which ultimately caused them to make the decision they did. This can provide answers and comfort to people in a very difficult time. This service can also help for purposes of life insurance policies and other legal matters.
